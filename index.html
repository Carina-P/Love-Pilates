What is Pilates?

Classic Pilates was invented a century ago by Joseph Pilates. He took inspiration from dance, strength training, yoga, martial arts, gymnastics but also movement patterns from animals.

Pilates is a training method that uses a special type of Pilates equipment (apparatus). In its simplest form, one uses only a thick exercise mat to improve strength, posture, flexibility and mental awareness.

Essential Pilates

Essential Pilates is the perfect class for learning the basics of Classic Pilates at a slower pace. As a beginner, you will learn the essential exercises in the original order with proper breathing and control. Mama Pilates
Wednesday at 1 pm
Mama Pilates

Bring your baby to class when you exercise! Postnatal Pilates classes for new mothers. The class focuses on the challenges women face after pregnancy and childbirth. More specifically, we will focus on training the deep abdominal muscles, pelvic floor and stretching the chest muscles and correcting posture. In addition, we train the trunk stability and upper body so you can easily lift your growing baby. You will feel stronger, longer and full of energy!

Pregnant Pilates

Pregnant Pilates

Pregnant Pilates is safe to perform during all three trimesters. You are more likely to experience back pain and to hurt yourself during pregnancy because of the shift of center of gravity in the body. You also carry a few extra pounds and your ligaments become softer and more unstable. With Pregnant Pilates focused and controlled exercises, you get an effective method to help you strengthen your postural and pelvic muscles which facilitates both labor and recovery after birth!
